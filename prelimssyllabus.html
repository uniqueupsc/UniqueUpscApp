UPSC Prelims Syllabus 2025

General Studies Paper-I

  • Current Events: National and international importance.
  • History of India and Indian National Movement: Ancient, medieval, and modern history; significant events and figures.
  • Indian and World Geography: Physical, social, and economic geography of India and the world.
  • Indian Polity and Governance: Constitution, political system, panchayati raj, public policy, rights issues.
  • Economic and Social Development: Sustainable development, poverty, inclusion, demographics, social sector initiatives.
  • Environmental Ecology, Biodiversity, and Climate Change: General issues without requiring subject specialization.
  • General Science: Physics, chemistry, biology, and their applications to daily life.

General Studies Paper-II (CSAT)

  • Comprehension: Understanding and interpreting written material.
  • Interpersonal Skills: Communication skills, including clarity and coherence.
  • Logical Reasoning and Analytical Ability: Identifying patterns, logical sequences, and problem-solving.
  • Decision-Making and Problem-Solving: Situational judgment and analytical decision-making.
  • General Mental Ability: Numerical and mathematical reasoning, data sufficiency.
  • Basic Numeracy: Numbers and their relations, orders of magnitude (up to Class X level).
  • Data Interpretation: Charts, graphs, tables, and data analysis (up to Class X level).
